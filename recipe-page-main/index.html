Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture, if you like.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: When the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan so the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the edges set but the center is slightly runny, sprinkle your fillings over one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Lift one edge and fold over the fillings. Cook another minute, then slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot and season to taste.
